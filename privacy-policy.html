ASTROFIC VPN Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 15, 2025
Last Updated: November 15, 2025

1. Introduction

ASTROFIC VPN ("we", "our", "the app") takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our VPN application (currently available for iOS).

By using ASTROFIC VPN, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Information We Do NOT Collect

ASTROFIC VPN follows a strict "No-Logs" policy. We DO NOT collect, store, or track:

  • Your IP address (we do not store IP addresses, but temporarily use them to determine country when requesting servers)
  • Browsing history
  • Content of your internet communications
  • DNS queries
  • Connection metadata
  • Session timestamps and duration
  • Precise geographic location (we only determine country by IP address when requesting servers and store this information)
  • Any other data that could identify you

2.2. Minimal Information Required for Service Operation

To provide VPN service, we collect the following minimal technical information:

  • Device Unique Identifier (Client UUID): Generated locally on your device and used for authentication when obtaining VPN server configuration
  • Country: Automatically determined by IP address on each server list request and stored on our servers to provide optimal servers in your region. The IP address itself is not stored, only the country determined from it.
  • Device Model: For compatibility and technical support
  • App Version: For compatibility and support purposes
  • iOS Operating System Version: For technical support and troubleshooting

This information is used solely for technical purposes and service operation.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for:

  • Providing and improving VPN service
  • Ensuring security and connection stability
  • Technical support and troubleshooting
  • Collecting anonymous usage statistics (number of active users, popular regions, app versions) to improve service quality
  • Compliance with legal obligations

We DO NOT use your information for:

  • Advertising or marketing
  • Selling to third parties
  • Creating user profiles for advertising
  • Tracking your internet activity or visited websites

4. Data Storage

4.1. No-Logs Policy

ASTROFIC VPN does not log your internet activity. We do not store records of:

  • Your connections
  • Websites visited
  • Data transmitted
  • Usage time

4.2. Local Storage

Some data is stored locally on your device:

  • Privacy policy acceptance status
  • Device unique identifier (Client UUID) - generated and stored locally
  • Selected VPN server
  • App settings (including Kill Switch setting)
  • Connection history (for display in the app)
  • Configuration name and last update date
  • Configuration hash for checking relevance

This data is stored only on your device in iOS secure storage. Connection history is stored locally and is not transmitted to our servers. Client UUID is transmitted only when requesting server configuration for authentication.

4.3. Retention Period

Technical information necessary for service operation (Client UUID, country, device model, app and iOS versions) is stored on our servers only while you use the service.

Automatic Data Deletion:

  • Data is automatically deleted if there have been no requests from your Client UUID to our servers for more than 30 days
  • After data deletion, you will be treated as a new user on your next use of the app

We do not log your internet activity, connection history, or visited websites.

5. Third-Party Data Sharing

ASTROFIC VPN DOES NOT sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. We do not share collected data (Client UUID, country, device model, app and iOS versions) with advertising networks, analytics services, or other commercial partners.

Exceptions are only:

  • When required by law or competent authorities
  • To protect our rights and security
  • With your explicit consent

6. Security

We employ modern security methods to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All connections are protected by OpenVPN encryption protocol
  • Secure Servers: Our VPN servers are hosted in secure data centers
  • Minimal Data Collection: We collect only minimally necessary information
  • Regular Updates: We regularly update the app to address security vulnerabilities

7. Your Rights

Under applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about what data we store about you
  • Correction: Correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data by sending a request to the email address provided in the "Contact" section. Data will be deleted within 30 days of receiving the request. Data is also automatically deleted if there have been no requests from your Client UUID for more than 30 days
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Portability: Receive a copy of your data in a structured format

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address provided in the "Contact" section.

8. Children

ASTROFIC VPN is intended for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age without parental consent. If we learn that a child under 13 is using our service without parental consent, we may suspend or terminate service to such person and delete associated information.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of significant changes:

  • By updating the "Last Updated" date at the beginning of this document
  • By publishing a new version of the policy on the documentation page
  • In the future, we plan to add notifications about changes directly in the app

Continued use of the app after changes are made indicates your agreement to the updated policy. We recommend periodically checking this page for updates.
